Who We Are

Built by a bettor.
Written for serious players.

Short Open started as a simple idea: most sports betting content is noise. Picks without process. Predictions without accountability. Numbers recycled from the same public models everyone else is already using.

We do it differently. Every piece of analysis starts with the raw data — player tendencies, line movement, situational splits, and market behavior. But data only tells part of the story. We also trust instinct — the kind the industry rarely accounts for. Pattern recognition built from years of watching games, not just charting them.

Our subscribers range from casual bettors looking for a sharper edge to experienced players who want a second set of eyes on their research. What they all have in common is that they take the process seriously.
